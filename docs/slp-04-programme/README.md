## Programme Summary

This 12-week SLP equips HR operations managers, departmental heads, service delivery managers, transformation teams, shared-services leaders, internal auditors, consultants, line managers and postgraduate learners to evaluate and design agentic AI and workflow automation in HRM.

The programme positions agentic AI as an emerging frontier. Learners build a practical **Agentic HRM Workflow Blueprint** that includes readiness assessment, workflow redesign, permissions, human oversight, audit trails and pilot evaluation.

## Delivery Assumption

The default design is online or blended distance learning for working adults. It assumes learners have basic digital literacy and process-management awareness, but not coding or AI engineering expertise.
