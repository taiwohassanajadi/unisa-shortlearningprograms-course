# Stakeholder Presentation Outline

## Slide 1: Title

**AI, Green HRM and Sustainable Work Transformation**

Subtitle: Proposed Short Learning Programme under CAS, Department of Auditing, Centre for Artificial Intelligence & Multidisciplinary Innovation Studies, and Centre for Lifelong Learning.

## Slide 2: Why This Programme, Why Now

- AI adoption and sustainability are increasingly connected.
- HRM is central to skills, behaviour, employee involvement and culture.
- Organisations need sustainable digital work, not only digital productivity.
- ESG and reporting expectations require better evidence.

## Slide 3: The Problem

Organisations often:

- Treat AI as only a productivity issue.
- Treat sustainability as separate from work design.
- Ignore AI's environmental and social trade-offs.
- Underinvest in green skills.
- Exclude employees from sustainable change.
- Report sustainability without enough workplace evidence.

## Slide 4: Target Learners

- HRM practitioners.
- Departmental heads.
- Sustainability managers.
- ESG teams.
- Organisational development practitioners.
- Learning and development teams.
- Public sector managers.
- Internal auditors.
- Consultants and postgraduate learners.

## Slide 5: Programme Promise

Learners will leave with a practical **Sustainable AI + HRM Transformation Plan** for a selected organisation, department or case.

## Slide 6: Programme Structure

- Week 1 to 3: Green HRM and green skills.
- Week 4 to 6: AI, digital work and environmental impact.
- Week 7 to 8: Sustainable work redesign and employee involvement.
- Week 9 to 11: Metrics, ESG alignment and implementation roadmap.
- Week 12: Final transformation plan presentation.

## Slide 7: Four Study Units

1. Green HRM and Sustainable Work.
2. AI, Digital Work and Environmental Impact.
3. Redesigning Work for Sustainable Performance.
4. Implementation, Metrics and ESG-Aligned Reporting.

## Slide 8: Assessment Model

- Green HRM practice audit: 10%.
- Sustainable AI use-case review: 15%.
- Green skills and employee involvement plan: 15%.
- Final Sustainable AI + HRM Transformation Plan: 60%.

## Slide 9: Responsible Sustainable AI + HRM

The programme integrates:

- Green HRM practices.
- Green skills development.
- Employee involvement.
- AI and digital work sustainability review.
- Responsible procurement questions.
- ESG-aligned metrics.
- Implementation roadmaps.

## Slide 10: Why CAS and Department of Auditing

- Sustainability claims require evidence.
- ESG-aligned reporting needs controls and assurance.
- AI adoption requires governance and accountability.
- The Department of Auditing strengthens metrics, evidence and reporting discipline.

## Slide 13: Closing Message

This SLP helps organisations connect AI-enabled work transformation with Green HRM, employee involvement and credible sustainability evidence.
