# Case Study and Worksheet Pack

## Case Study 1: Sustainable Digital HR Service Delivery

### Scenario

A public university HR department wants to digitise several paper-heavy HR processes and introduce AI-assisted employee self-service. Leaders expect faster service and less paper use, but employees worry about access, privacy and loss of human support.

### Learner Task

Prepare a Sustainable AI + HRM review that includes:

- Green HRM opportunities.
- AI and digital work risks.
- Employee involvement plan.
- Green skills needs.
- Metrics and reporting.

### Discussion Questions

- What sustainability benefits are realistic?
- Which employees may be excluded?
- What support should remain human?
- What evidence should be reported?

## Case Study 2: Green Skills for AI-Enabled Work

### Scenario

An organisation is adopting AI tools for reporting, planning and operational decision support. Managers understand productivity benefits but have not considered sustainability, digital wellbeing or responsible tool use.

### Learner Task

Design a green skills and employee involvement plan.

### Discussion Questions

- What green skills do managers need?
- How should employees be trained on responsible AI use?
- What behaviours should be encouraged?
- How can learning outcomes be measured?

## Case Study 3: Sustainable Hybrid Work

### Scenario

A department uses hybrid work to reduce commuting and office space pressure. However, digital meetings have increased, employees report fatigue and managers struggle to coordinate work fairly.

### Learner Task

Redesign the hybrid work process using productivity, wellbeing, inclusion and sustainability lenses.

### Discussion Questions

- What sustainability benefits are real, and what is assumed?
- How can digital overload be reduced?
- What inclusion risks exist?
- What should managers measure?
