## Programme Summary

This 12-week SLP equips HRM practitioners, departmental heads, sustainability managers, ESG teams, organisational development practitioners, learning and development teams, public sector managers, internal auditors, consultants and postgraduate learners to connect AI adoption with Green HRM and sustainable work transformation.

The programme teaches learners to design a practical **Sustainable AI + HRM Transformation Plan** covering Green HRM practice, green skills, employee involvement, sustainable AI use, ESG alignment and responsible digital work.

## Delivery Assumption

The default design is online or blended distance learning for working adults. It assumes learners have basic digital literacy and management awareness, but not coding, data science or environmental engineering expertise.
