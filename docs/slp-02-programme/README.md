## Programme Summary

This 12-week SLP equips HR leaders, departmental heads, compliance practitioners, internal auditors, data protection officers, labour relations practitioners, public sector managers, consultants and postgraduate learners to govern AI use in employment and people-management contexts.

The programme translates responsible AI principles, POPIA obligations, risk management frameworks and audit-style controls into practical HRM governance artefacts. Learners build a workplace-ready **Responsible AI Governance Pack for HRM** as the final portfolio.

## Delivery Assumption

The default design is online or blended distance learning for working adults. It assumes learners have basic digital literacy and workplace governance awareness, but not coding or technical AI model-building expertise.
